About

YUVA Hackathon is a 24-hour onsite innovation sprint hosted by SRMIST Trichy, bringing together brilliant minds to solve real-world challenges across themes like Healthcare, Agritech, LangChain, and Open Innovation. Compete, collaborate, and build impactful tech solutions to win prizes worth ₹50,000 and exciting swags!

Structure

Round 1 – PPT Submission:

Teams submit their idea presentation before the deadline.

Round 2 – Shortlisting & Payment:

Shortlisted teams will receive an official email and must pay the registration fee to confirm participation.

Round 3 – Onsite Hackathon:

Finalists are invited to SRMIST Trichy for a 24-hour offline hackathon, where they build and present working prototypes.

Timeline

PPT Submission Deadline: August 28, 2025

Shortlisted Teams Announced: September 5, 2025

Onsite Hackathon Starts: September 16, 2025 at 11:00 AM

Hackathon Ends & Prize Distribution: September 17, 2025

Rules

  • Each team must consist of 3–5 members.
  • Registration fee applies only to shortlisted teams.
  • Only pay when contacted officially.
  • No transportation provided.
  • Rest zones will be available.
  • Bring your own hardware materials (10 days prep).
  • Food stalls available on campus.
  • Carry own chargers, extension boxes, etc.
  • Plagiarism leads to disqualification.
  • Judging panel includes experts from PayPal, Flutter, etc.
